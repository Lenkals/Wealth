Retirement benefits
worth knowing about

India's retirement framework offers multiple layers of financial protection. Here's what you're entitled to — and how to maximise every rupee.

🏛️

NPS — National Pension System

Invest during your earning years, receive a lifelong annuity at retirement. Contributions up to ₹2 lakh p.a. attract dual tax deductions under Sec 80C & 80CCD(1B). The equity component delivers market-linked growth.

🏦

EPF & EPS — Employee Provident Fund

Your employer matches 12% of your basic salary. After 10 years of service, EPS entitles you to a monthly pension for life. At 58, tax-free withdrawal of the full EPF corpus — one of the most powerful retirement tools available.

🧾

PPF — Public Provident Fund

15-year compounding at government-guaranteed rates with complete EEE (exempt-exempt-exempt) tax status. Ideal as a debt anchor in your retirement portfolio. Extensions in blocks of 5 years after maturity.

🏥

Senior Citizen Healthcare Benefits

After 60, you're entitled to higher interest rates on savings, Ayushman Bharat coverage, PMVVY pension scheme access, and significant income tax relief — including a ₹50,000 standard deduction and higher exemption thresholds.

🏠

Reverse Mortgage & Housing

If you own a home, a reverse mortgage lets you draw monthly income against it without selling. For affordable housing owners, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana extensions provide continued housing security well into retirement.

📈

Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)

Guaranteed returns of ~8.2% p.a. with quarterly payouts — up to ₹30 lakh per depositor. Best-in-class post-tax yield for retirees seeking predictable, government-backed cash flow without equity risk.
